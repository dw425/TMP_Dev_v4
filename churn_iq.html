Summary

A Director of Customer Experience at a major gaming platform has made a strategic investment in Databricks to reduce customer churn and improve player lifetime value. Her responsibility is twofold: proactively identifying at-risk players before they churn, and validating that targeted retention strategies are driving measurable business outcomes.

What It Does

Forecast churn and personalize retention at scale

Churn Forecasting & Risk Segmentation: ChurnIQ helps you understand which users are likely to churn and why—based on real behavioral patterns, not just assumptions. It builds predictive models that segment users by churn risk, generating actionable insights for marketing and product teams.

Retention Strategy Personalization: Move beyond generic offers. ChurnIQ enables you to generate and test tailored next-best actions—such as targeted promotions, in-game rewards, or product nudges—aligned specifically to user segments.

Measurement & Accountability: Track the performance of every action and tie it back to measurable changes in churn, engagement, and revenue. This solution provides visibility into how teams are using insights and validates that interventions are happening fast enough to make a difference.
